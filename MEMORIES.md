# A Little Piece of Berlin

> *A little piece of Berlin, preserved one commit at a time.*

Some people keep photographs.

I kept repositories.

This repository is more than a collection of assignments and projects. It is a quiet reminder of a chapter of my life spent learning, exploring, and growing at **Charité – Universitätsmedizin Berlin**.

Every notebook tells a story. Every project brings back a memory. Every commit marks another lesson learned—not just in programming, but in life.

One day these technologies will be outdated, and the code may no longer run. But I hope the memories never fade.

If you're reading this years from now, I hope this repository reminds you of the excitement of discovering something new, the long evenings spent debugging, the conversations between lectures, and the privilege of calling Berlin home, even if only for a little while.

**Thank you, Berlin.**
